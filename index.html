Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan referral services.

What types of loans do you offer?

We connect clients with lenders offering personal loans for various purposes including debt consolidation, home improvements, major purchases, and more. Loan amounts typically range from $5,000 to $50,000 depending on qualification.

How does your referral service work?

We collect your information and loan requirements, then match you with lenders in our network who may meet your needs. You'll have the opportunity to review potential offers and choose the one that works best for you.

Is there a fee for your service?

Our loan referral service is free to use. We receive compensation from our lending partners when you successfully secure a loan through our referrals.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

Our network includes lenders with various credit requirements. While some lenders may require good to excellent credit scores, others specialize in working with borrowers with fair or rebuilding credit.

How long does the process take?

After submitting your application, you can typically expect to receive potential matches within 1-2 business days. Once you select a lender and complete their requirements, funding can often be completed within 3-7 business days.
